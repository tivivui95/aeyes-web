Our Story

When we studied in the high school, we saw the struggle of blind have to face with everyday, this is the reason why we want to do the product to serve the large amount of blind and we want to bring the message to the world “Technology can help the disability people to have the better life”.

Motivation

Our product focus on the problem that everyone face in the daily life. We hope we can bring the positive impact to the society and also we like the challenge and want to introduct Vietnamese technology to the world.
